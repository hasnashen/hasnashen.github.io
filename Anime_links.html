✧ These are all about my favourite anime ✧

I enjoy watching anime on weekends and holidays. All kinds of genres grab my interest because the characters are so vivid and unique. In action anime, each character has amazing magical or supernatural abilities. In detective anime, the characters are keen observers, with Conan being a classic example. The stories between the main characters under sakura (cherry blossom trees) in romance anime are also very warm and romantic. There are many other types as well. By watching them, I feel as if I'm personally experiencing the lives of people in another dimension, a world different from my own. Sometimes, I cosplay my favorite characters, just like stepping into their roles.
