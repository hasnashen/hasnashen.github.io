My family consists of my parents, my younger sister, and me. My father is a talented man who is great at drawing and cooking. He's a fixer-upper with a fun sense of humor, always making time to play with us. My mother is also a gifted artist and a true artist in the kitchen, specializing in delicious desserts. She is very caring, always looking after our health and our outfits. I enjoy chatting, joking and going shopping with her (even though we usually have different ideas)..My creative younger sister is a mix of adorable and annoying--- we fight sometimes，but we also play together and share countless jokes. I deeply admire her social skills. Overall, I just love being with my family.
